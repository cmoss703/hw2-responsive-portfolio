I grew up in a small Kansas town, graduated with a degree in Architectural Engineering from the University of Kansas, and moved to Kansas City, MO where I currently work as a Fire Protection Engineer and Building Life Safety Code Consultant. Fire Protection can be pretty interesting at times.
